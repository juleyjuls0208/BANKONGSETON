<objective>
Fix two independent bugs in `backend/api/api_server.py`:

1. **REQ-BUG-01 — Double-spend race condition**: Two simultaneous requests for the same card both read the same balance before either writes, causing one deduction to be silently lost. Fix: per-card `threading.Lock()` guards the full read-check-write sequence in both payment endpoints.

2. **REQ-BUG-04 — Email crash after commit**: The email block in `process_cashier_transaction()` runs after the balance is already deducted. If `email_service.send_receipt()` raises, the outer `except Exception` returns a 500 — even though the money is gone. Fix: wrap the email block in its own `try/except Exception` with a `logger.warning()`, no re-raise.

Purpose: Prevent silent balance corruption and phantom 500s on successful transactions.
Output: Modified `backend/api/api_server.py` with lock infrastructure + email guard.
</objective>
