<objective>
Add RF field toggle, UID-length dispatch, and diagnostic printing to the R3 Arduino sketch
(bankongseton_nfc_r3.ino) so NTAG215 physical cards are detected and reported correctly.

Purpose: R3 is missing all three features that R4 already has. Without the RF toggle,
NTAG215 never powers up. Without UID-length dispatch, NTAG215 cards enter the APDU path
(which fails for 7-byte UIDs) instead of emitting `CARD|<uid>` directly. Without the
diagnostic print, no-detection events are silent on serial (hard to debug).

Output: Modified `bankongseton_nfc_r3.ino` with 4 additions to loop(). No new files.
</objective>
