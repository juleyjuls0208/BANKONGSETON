<objective>
Fix the PN532 RF field configuration in the R4 Arduino sketch (bankongseton_rfid.ino) so
NTAG215 physical student cards are detected in passive ISO14443A mode.

Purpose: `setRFField(1, 1)` (autoRFCA=1) is for NFC peer-to-peer active mode, not passive
card reading. With autoRFCA=1, the PN532 suppresses its own RF field during passive sensing,
so NTAG215 never powers up → NbTg=0 → card never detected. Two surgical changes fix this.
Removing `setPassiveActivationRetries(0x0A)` eliminates a secondary blocker where 10 retries
are insufficient for NTAG215's 7-byte double-cascade anticollision.

Output: Modified `bankongseton_rfid.ino` with 2 one-line fixes + 1 removed call.
</objective>
